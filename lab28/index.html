Who I am?

The coolest kawaii bunny around, MOLANG was born in Seoul in 2010. South Korean illustrator Hye-Ji Yoon doodled MOLANG and PIU PIU on her school notebooks before deciding to turn the characters into super-cute emoticons online. Four years later, a chance encounter between Hye-Ji and French animation studio Millimages resulted in a cross-cultural love affair for the undeniably cuddly bunny. The studio couldn’t resist but pair Molang with the adorable chick Piu Piu, unknowingly creating a match made in heaven.
